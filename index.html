About Us

More Than Just a Car Wash.

We’re not a franchise, we’re your neighbors. Since 2003, the Ardsley Car Wash has been a fixture on Saw Mill River Road, built on a simple belief: treat every car like it’s your own and treat every customer like family.

Over 20 years of early mornings, rainy days, and regulars who’ve become friends. We’re proud to be part of this community sponsoring local schools, supporting town events, and showing up for Southern Westchester the same way Southern Westchester has always shown up for us.
